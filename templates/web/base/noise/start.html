To report noise you will need:

  • Your address
  • A phone number
  • An idea of the source of the noise

After your report:

  • We’ll contact you for more details
  • We might want to visit your home to observe the noise

Details:

  • Your report is confidential and we won’t share your details with anyone
  • Noise complaints can take a long time to resolve; we will do our best to keep you updated as the case progresses
  • For further action to be taken over noise nuisance it has to be deemed a 'statutory nuisance', that materially affects the comfort of your home. It should be loud enough to wake you up at night or prevent you from concentrating during the day. One off incidents are unlikely to be considered a nuisance.

Out of hours

Should the noise occur between 6.30pm and 02:00am on a Thursday, 9.00pm and 05.00am on a Friday / Saturday, or 6.30pm and 02.00am on Sunday, please contact the out of hours team on 020 8356 4455. If the noise is ongoing, we will visit you to try and witness the nuisance (other priorities permitting) and if necessary, take action to stop it.
