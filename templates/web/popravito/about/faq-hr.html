Što je ova stranica?

Je li ovo internetska stranica lokalne samouprave?

Ne, nije - ali Popravi.to vaše prijave šalje izravno jedinici lokalne (područne) samouprave (JL(P)S) odnosno nadležnoj službi.

Također sve objavljujemo na internetu, tako da drugi u zajednici mogu čitati, raspravljati i po potrebi pružati savjete.

Tko ste onda vi? I zašto to radite?

Popravi.to je neovisna internetska stranica koju održava inicijativa Code for Croatia i organizacija civilnog društva Gong. Želimo olakšati prijavljivanje problema u zajednici, čak i ako ne znate kome bi te prijave trebale biti upućene.

Tako smo napravili Popravi.to. Sve što trebate je unijeti adresu - ili dopustiti internetskom pregledniku da vas automatski pronađe - i opisati svoj problem. Popravi.to vašu prijavu šalje ljudima čiji je posao to popraviti.

Popravi.to pokriva cijelu Hrvatsku. Bez obzira gdje se nalazili, jedina stvar koju morate znati je adresa naše internetske stranice - Popravi.to.

Using FixMyStreet

How do I make a report?

  1. Start at the homepage and type in your postcode or a place name. Not sure where you are? Pick ‘use my location’.
  2. Put the pin in the map to show exactly where the problem is, and type in a description of the issue. Then send.
  3. Check your email inbox for our confirmation mail, click on the link, and job done.

What can I report?

FixMyStreet is an independent website, built by the charity mySociety. We wanted to make it easier to report problems in your community, even if you don't know who those reports should go to.

So we made FixMyStreet. All you have to do is type in a postcode – or let the site locate you automatically – and describe your problem. Then we send your report to the people whose job it is to fix it.

FixMyStreet covers the whole of the UK. No matter where you are, the only thing you ever have to remember is our web address – FixMyStreet.com. Do councils read or act on FixMyStreet reports?

They certainly do. Reports sent through FixMyStreet go into the same council email inboxes as reports sent through by any other means.

Really smart councils integrate directly, so we can place report details directly into their systems, saving them time and money.
